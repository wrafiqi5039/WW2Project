About

On September 1st, 1939, Germany invaded Poland. This attack by Germany would undoubtedly lead to Britain and France, to declare war on Germany and that would start the Second World War. The Second World War was one of the largest and most deadliest conflict in human history. More than 60 million people lost their lives during this conflict. There were fighting all throughout the world, such fronts all over Europe, Asia, and Africa. There fighting throughout the Pacific and Atlantic Ocean.
